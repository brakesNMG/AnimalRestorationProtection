Protecting Wildlife

We focus on protecting animals and their well-being. We respond to reports of animals in need, support injured animals, and work with communities to build long-term stewardship.

Our Mission

Protect species by rescuing, rehabilitating, and conserving wildlife — so animals can thrive now and into the future.

Programs

  • Injured Animal Reporting — crowd-sourced reports to help rescue and care for injured or distressed animals.
  • Rescue & Rehabilitation — volunteer support for rescues, injured animal care, and partnerships with rehab centers.
  • Community Science — monitoring species and ecosystem health to guide actions.
  • Education & Outreach — workshops, school programs, and stewardship training.

How You Can Help

  • Report injured or distressed animals using the Report form.
  • Volunteer for local events and monitoring.
  • Share our mission — spread the word and support conservation-friendly policies.
